Welcome to Johnson

Comprehensive care built around each patient.

From evaluation and fitting to education, adjustments, and long-term follow-up, our focus is helping patients move safely and confidently.

Custom Prosthetics

Evaluation, fitting, and follow-up support for prosthetic devices designed for comfort, alignment, and function.

Pediatric Prosthetic Care

Supportive care for children and families, with attention to growth, confidence, and continued adjustment needs.

Orthotic & Bracing Solutions

Bracing and orthotic support designed to improve stability, positioning, comfort, and daily function.

Care that supports the whole journey.

Patients deserve to feel heard, respected, and guided through each step of the prosthetic or orthotic process.

  • Patient-centered evaluation based on lifestyle, mobility, and clinical needs
  • Custom fitting with attention to comfort, alignment, and function
  • Education for patients, caregivers, and families
  • Ongoing follow-up to support long-term mobility
  • Insurance and referral coordination handled by our team
Our goal is to help every patient feel supported, capable, and ready for the next step.

Personalized Design

Devices as unique as you are.

Your prosthetic is part of you — and we believe it should reflect that. Patients can request custom artwork, finishes, religious or scripture-based designs, sports themes, military pride, and personalized colors. Your story, your way.

From a quiet finish to a hand-detailed scripture wrap like this one, every custom design is finished by hand in our in-house workshop.

★ Family-Owned & Operated

Meet Greg & Carolyn.

Johnson Prosthetics & Orthotics is owned and operated by Greg and Carolyn Johnson. Greg has spent his career as a certified prosthetist and orthotist, and together they built this clinic to give patients the kind of attention and continuity larger practices simply can't offer.

Every patient relationship runs through them personally — from the first phone call to long-term follow-up — and that's exactly the way they want it. Not a corporate chain. A husband-and-wife team that believes patients deserve to feel known by the people designing their devices.
